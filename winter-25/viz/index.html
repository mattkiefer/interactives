Storytelling with data viz

In the previous module, we covered some of the benefits of data-driven journalism. Citing your evidence and explaining your methodology can help you gain credibility and trust.

There are also benefits to engagement and accessibility. Visual formats can do a better job of drawing audience attention. Data viz can work for everyone: it can condense a complicated story into a simple chart for those who prefer a quick summary -- or it can offer endless options for users to search and interact with specific filters and data points.
